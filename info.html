Game Info
Controls:
Player № 1: W, A, S, D
Player № 2: ArrowUp, ArrowLeft, ArrowDown, ArrowRight
Description:
This is a survival race for two players. The one who scores the most points in 3 rounds wins!
To earn points, you need to dodge enemies that will constantly be coming at you.
To start the game, select the 'Start' tab and press the Enter key.
Next, each player can choose the car they want to play with.
There are a total of 4 types of cars in the game, each with its own speed.
You can view the specifications in the menu under the 'Garage' tab.
The more points players score, the harder the game will become.
The speed of the game will continuously increase, and new enemies will appear.
I wish you a successful and fun game!
